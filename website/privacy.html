Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy covers Trailguide ("we", "us", "our"), including the Trailguide web application at app.gettrailguide.com and the Trailguide Recorder Chrome Extension.

1. Trailguide Recorder Chrome Extension

The Trailguide Recorder extension helps you create product tours by recording steps on your web app. Here is exactly what it collects:

  • Screenshots — when you click an element during a recording session, a screenshot of the visible tab is captured and sent to the Trailguide editor to serve as a visual reference for that step.
  • CSS selectors — the CSS selector of the element you clicked is captured and sent to the editor so the tour can target that element in production.

This data is transmitted only to the Trailguide editor running in your browser session. It is not sent to any third party. Screenshots are stored in your browser's local storage and, if you are a Pro subscriber, in your private Supabase storage bucket.

The extension does not collect browsing history, personally identifiable information, passwords, financial data, location, or any data outside of an active recording session you initiate.

2. Trailguide Web Application

When you create an account at app.gettrailguide.com:

  • Account information — your email address and OAuth identity (GitHub or GitLab) are stored to authenticate your account.
  • Trail data — the product tours you create, including step content and screenshots, are stored in your account.
  • Usage analytics — if you enable analytics on your tours, aggregated event data (tour starts, completions, step views) from your end users is stored and displayed in your dashboard. No personally identifiable information about your end users is collected.
  • Payment information — billing is handled by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment credentials.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Trailguide service.
  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription.
  • To store and sync your product tours.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Stripe — payment processing.

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@gettrailguide.com.
